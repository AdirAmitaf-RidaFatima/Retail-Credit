# Module Documentation

## Executive Dashboard

Purpose: Give senior management a 30-second view of portfolio risk and economics.

Core questions:
- How large is the portfolio?
- What is the average PD?
- How much expected loss exists?
- Is the portfolio profitable after credit losses?
- Which facilities, tenors, or geographies create concentration risk?

## Borrower Intelligence

Purpose: Support underwriters in reviewing borrower-level risk and creating a credit memo.

The page displays PD, risk band, EL, expected return, recommendation, applicant characteristics, risk drivers, and mitigants.

## Portfolio Analytics

Purpose: Support risk policy and portfolio management through segment-level analysis.

Key dimensions: facility, tenor, grade, sub-grade, purpose, employment, home ownership, geography, verification status, risk band, decision.

## Credit Decision Simulator

Purpose: Show how credit economics change when borrower or facility assumptions change.

The simulator uses the original model PD as an anchor and applies transparent sensitivity adjustments. It is a scenario tool, not a replacement for live model scoring.

## Model Intelligence

Purpose: Provide model governance evidence: performance, calibration, confusion matrices, feature importance, and SHAP output.

## Risk & Portfolio Monitoring

Purpose: Identify high-risk exposure, negative-return accounts, risk concentration, and vintage-style risk migration.

## Reports & Export

Purpose: Provide portable outputs for committees, audit packs, and demonstrations.
